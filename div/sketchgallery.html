World of Blackness, Whiteness, and Symmetry

Welcome to World of Blackness, Whiteness, and Symmetry!
Black and white are two interesting colors and are regarded as one of the best combinations. Especially, when these two colors are combined in the form of symmetry, it reveals the power of simplicity. Hope you can explore the World of Blackness, Whiteness, and Symmetry with your great creativity!
By choosing one of the numbers from 0 to 4, you will get a different and unique interactive experience playing with blackness, whiteness, and symmetry. Basically, geometry goes through eight or four quadrant symmetry.
  • By choosing 0, you will play with dots. It's quite easy for you to generate beautiful images!
  • By choosing 1, you will play with the dots of different sizes.
  • By choosing 2, you will play with squares of different sizes and transparency.
  • By choosing 3, you will engage with the line.
  • By choosing 4, you will engage with strange lines. When you want to draw a vertical line, a horizontal line appears.
